# Brand Direction

## Core mood
Premium, intelligent, tactile, cinematic, product-led.

## Reference
- Structural inspiration: https://kanesherwell.com/

## Visual language
- Liquid glass
- Restrained glassmorphism
- Subtle skeuomorphic tactility
- Editorial layout rhythm
- Dark-first premium palette
- Selective 3D depth

## Avoid
- Generic SaaS cards
- Blue-purple AI gradients
- Over-centered sections
- Glowing blob backgrounds
- Template-looking bento grids everywhere
